COMPANY FORMATION

We offer you support and guidance in forming your own company, every step of the way. you can rest assured that you are in safe hands. Whether you are an intermediary representing a group of clients or an entrepreneur setting up for the first time, you can expect from us an unrivalled quality of service.

VAT

Prepare and provide excellent VAT services to clients. Advice and apply the suitable VAT Scheme for the clients businesses. Compute the correct VAT due using the right scheme. Submit the approved completed VAT return online on behalf of clients.

PAYROLL AND PAYE

All PAYE and payroll matters. Preparation of weekly or monthly Payslips for Employees and Directors. Preparation pay and deduction statements for subcontractors. Preparation of CIS monthly and annual returns for contractors and their online submissions to HMRC.

PERSONAL INCOME TAX

Prepare clients self-assessment returns and submit them online. Assist and advise clients on various personal tax matters including Tax investigations, enquiries and interventions. Tax Refunds and Repayment claims for clients including subcontractors tax repayment claims. Represent clients on dispute resolutions with H. M. Revenue and Customs. Tribunal Representations and case handling

CORPORATION TAX

General advice and assistance in compliance with corporation tax matters of the company. Filing the corporation tax returns online for the company including joint filing with companies house.

BUSINESS INCOME TAX
